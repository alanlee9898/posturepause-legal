The app

Posture Pause provides gentle local reminders to check your posture during work hours. It is a habit tool, not a medical device.

Subscriptions & lifetime purchase

  • Yearly Premium — auto-renewable. Free trial when offered by Apple. Cancel in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
  • Monthly Premium — auto-renewable with the same Premium features.
  • Lifetime Premium — one-time purchase unlocking Premium permanently on that Apple ID.

Premium unlocks: all reminder intervals, streaks & history detail, stretch library, themes, and custom reminder messages.
