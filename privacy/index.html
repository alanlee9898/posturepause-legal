Privacy Policy

Posture Pause · Last updated: July 17, 2026

What stays on your device

Reminder settings, daily correction counts, and streak metadata are stored locally on your iPhone or iPad. We do not require an account.

Purchases

If you buy Premium, Apple processes payment. RevenueCat verifies the subscription entitlement so Premium features unlock across reinstalls with the same Apple ID. We do not receive your card details.

What we do not collect

  • No advertising or tracking identifiers for ads
  • No analytics SDKs other than RevenueCat for subscriptions
  • No camera, microphone, motion, or location data in version 1.0
  • No iCloud sync in version 1.0
